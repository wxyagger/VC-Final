Luiza Dale Website

Luiza Dale is a graphic designer living in Brooklyn who works on commissions for artists, cultural organizations, design agencies, and publishers encompassing identity systems, websites, and print publications. Collaborators include: Buffy, CUP, Day End Records, Kin Euphorics, Newest York Arts Press, Picture Room, Salad for President, Yale CCAM, Yale University Art Gallery, Yale School of Art, and Yale School of Architecture.
